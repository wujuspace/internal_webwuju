FEATURED PROJECTS

Superpixel Christmas Greeting Card AR

Superpixel AR

An augmented reality greeting card that brings festive celebrations to life through interactive 3D animations and immersive holiday experiences. This innovative project transforms traditional holiday greetings into magical digital moments, where recipients can unlock animated scenes, interactive games, and personalized messages through their smartphones, creating lasting memories that extend the joy of the holiday season.

Molca Factory

Molca Teknologi Nusantara

A comprehensive digital transformation solution for manufacturing facilities, integrating smart technologies and data visualization for enhanced operations. This enterprise-level project combines IoT sensors, real-time analytics, and intuitive dashboard interfaces to provide factory managers with unprecedented visibility into production processes, equipment performance, and operational efficiency, enabling data-driven decision-making that optimizes output and reduces downtime.

Ajinomoto - Digital Twin

Ajinomoto Digital Twin

Creating a virtual replica of production facilities enabling real-time monitoring, predictive analytics, and optimized manufacturing processes. This digital twin technology allows stakeholders to visualize entire manufacturing operations in real-time, simulate scenarios, identify bottlenecks before they occur, and make informed decisions that improve productivity, reduce costs, and ensure consistent quality across all production lines.

AR Panjat Pinang - Superpixel

Superpixel Independence Day

An augmented reality adaptation of traditional Indonesian Independence Day celebration, bringing cultural heritage into the digital age with interactive experiences. This culturally significant project preserves traditional games and celebrations while making them accessible to modern audiences through AR technology, allowing users to participate in virtual versions of classic Independence Day activities while learning about Indonesian history and cultural values.

Re-Route Fest Singapore 2024

Reroute Fest

A festival experience combining music, technology, and art through innovative digital installations and immersive audience engagement platforms. This multi-sensory event design created unforgettable moments where festival-goers could interact with synchronized light displays, reactive soundscapes, and participatory art installations that responded to their movements, creating a unique fusion of technology and creativity that elevated the entire festival experience.

Toyota Astra Motor Experience

TAM Environment

Revolutionizing automotive retail through immersive showroom experiences that blend physical and digital interactions for enhanced customer engagement. Through configurable vehicle displays, virtual test drives, and personalized customization interfaces, customers can explore every aspect of their potential purchase in an engaging, interactive environment that makes car shopping an enjoyable and informative experience rather than a transactional encounter.

Intive Studio Branding

Intive Studio

A comprehensive brand identity system that reflects innovation and creativity across all digital touchpoints and physical spaces. This holistic rebrand encompassed everything from logo design and color palettes to website architecture, office environments, and marketing materials, ensuring a consistent and compelling brand experience that communicates the studio's values and expertise at every interaction point.

Bank International Portal

Bank International

A modern banking platform designed for seamless user experience, combining functionality with intuitive design for enhanced financial services. This comprehensive digital banking solution streamlines complex financial transactions while maintaining security and compliance, allowing customers to manage accounts, investments, and services through an interface that feels both powerful and approachable, making sophisticated banking tools accessible to users of all technical backgrounds.

Universitas Airlangga VR Experience

UNAIR VR

An educational virtual reality platform that transforms learning experiences through immersive simulations and interactive academic environments. This groundbreaking educational tool transports students into realistic scenarios that would be impossible to recreate in traditional classrooms, from exploring historical events first-hand to conducting virtual scientific experiments, revolutionizing how knowledge is acquired and retained through experiential learning.
